# Commercial Pilot Certificate

## Eligibility

[61.123]() | [141 App D]()

- hold a PPC
- age 18
- speak English
- ground training and endorsement
- pass knowledge test
- flight training and endorsement
- pass practical test

### Part 141

- hold an Instrument Rating

## Flight Experience

[61.129]()

- 250 hours total
- 50 hours xc
- 20 hours foggle
- 10 hours solo
- 10 hours complex, turbine, or TAA
- 5 hours night
- 2 hour 100 nm xc day
- 2 hour 100 nm xc night 
- 300 nm xc
- 10 night towered landings
- 3 hours dual checkride prep

### Part 141

- 35 hours ground
- 120 hours total
- 55 hours dual
- 10 hours foggle (only)
- 250 nm xc (only)

## Privileges

[61.133]()

Act as PIC of an aircraft carrying persons or property for compensation or hire

## Limitations

[61.133]()

### No Instrument Rating

Cannot carry passengers

- in excess of 50 nm
- at night

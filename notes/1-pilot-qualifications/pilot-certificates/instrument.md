# Instrument Rating

## Eligibility

[61.65]() | [141 App C]() | [Syllabus]()

- hold a PPL
- speak English
- 30 hrs ground 
- 35 hrs flight
    + up to 40% can be in an AATD
- 1 cross country
    + 250 nm total
    + 100 nm straight line distance between at least 2 airports
    + 3 approaches, at least 1 at each airport

## Privileges

[61.3]()

Act as PIC under IFR and in conditions less than VFR

[91.135]()

Operate in Class A airspace

[91.157]()

Operate in Special VFR at night

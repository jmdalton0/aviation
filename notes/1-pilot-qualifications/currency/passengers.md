# Currency For Taking Passengers

[61.57]()

## Day

To act as PIC of an aircraft either

- carrying passengers
- certificated for more than one pilot flight crewmember

A pilot must perform

- 3 takeoffs and landings
- in the preceding 90 days
- as sole manipulator of the flight controls
- in the same category, class, and type

## Night

If the flight happens between 1 hour after sunset and 1 hour before sunrise, the landings must be 

- 1 hour after sunset
- 1 hour before sunrise
- full stop

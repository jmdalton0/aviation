# Currency

## Currency vs Proficiency

**Currency** - certified and legal

**Proficiency** - safe and skilled

### Proficient But Not Current

- you have 50 night landings in the last week, but 0 to a full stop
- you have 500 hours actual in the last 6 months, but 0 holds

### Current But Not Proficient

- on your last flight you did 3 takeoffs and landings, but it was 89 days ago
- you recently earned your instrument rating, but you have 0 hours actual

## Flight Review

[61.56]()

To act as PIC, a pilot must perform, within the last 24 calendar months, a flight review

- review of current and applicable operating rules, manuevers, and procedures
- 1 hour ground minimum
- 1 hour flight minimum
- logbook endorsement from authorized instructor

A flight review can be substituded with

- practical test for a certificate or rating

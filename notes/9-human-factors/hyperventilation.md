# Hyperventilation

[PHAK 17]()

**Hyperventilation** - breathing faster and deeper than normal causes a lack of C02

Hyperventilation is dangerous because it can lead to unconsiousness.

## Causes

Hyperventilation is caused by stressful situations where the pilot subconsciously increases their rate of breathing.

## Syptoms

- lightheadedness 
- dizziness
- visual impairment
- tingling in fingers or toes
- hot and cold sensations
- muscle spasms
- unconsciousness

## Corrections

- reduce stress
- intentionally slow breathing
- talk out loud or sing
- breath into a paper bag

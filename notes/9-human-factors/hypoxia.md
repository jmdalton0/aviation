# Hypoxia

[PHAK 17]()

**Hypoxia** - lack of oxygen

Hypoxia is dangerous because it reduces our mental function. 

## Causes

### Hypoxic Hypoxia

- lack of oxygen to the body as a whole
- casued by choking, drowning, increased altitude

**Note** - the percentage of oxygen at altitude is constant, but the partial pressure decreases proportionaly to the atmospheric pressure

### Hypemic Hypoxia

- lack of blood
- caused by CO poisoning, blood loss or donation, anemia

### Histotoxic Hypoxia

- cells cannot use oxygen
- typically because of alcohol, drugs, poison

### Stagnant Hypoxia

- body cannot transport blood
- typically because of excessive Gs, bad circulation, shock

## Symptoms

- headache
- lightheadedness 
- dizziness
- drowsiness
- impaired judgement
- increased reaction time
- decreased response to stimuli
- visual impairment
- euphoria
- cyanosis (blue fingernails or lips)
- tingling in fingers or toes
- numbness
- unconsciousness

## Corrections

- descend quickly
- use supplemental oxygen

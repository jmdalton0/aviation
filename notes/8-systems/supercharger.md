# Supercharger

[PHAK 7]()

**Supercharger** - device that increases the power output of an engine by compressing air before it enters the cylinders

## Operation

- the **engine** drives an **air compressor**
- air enters the intake valve and is compressed
- compressed air enters the intake manifold where it mixes with fuel before entering the cylinders

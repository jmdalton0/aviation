# Special Use Airspace

[AIM 3-4]()

### Prohibited Areas

- prohibited for security or national welfare.

### Restricted Areas

- prohibited, when active
- hazardous because of military operations
- when inactive, ATC becomes the controlling body and may route IFR traffic through the area


### Warning Areas

- extend 3nm past the coast
- hazardous because of military operations
- U.S. cannot regulate them because they are over international waters

### Military Operations Areas

- for military arial training 
- hazardous because of military operations

### Controlled Firing Areas

- will stop activity when traffic is detected.

### Alert Areas

- contain a high volume of training or unusual activities.

### National Security Areas

- contain ground facilites with increased security or risk.
- pilots are requested to avoid

## Entry

Prohibited - none 

Restricted - none, unless inactive and cleared by ATC

Warning - allowed, but dangerous if active

MOA - allowed, but dangerous if active

CFA - allowed 

Alert - allowed 

NSA - requested to avoid

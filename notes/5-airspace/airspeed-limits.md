# Airspeed Limits

## Anywhere in the U.S. 

[91.817]()

Mach 1

## Below 10,000 MSL

[91.117]()

250 KIAS

## Specific Airspaces

[91.117]()

- within 4 nm of a class C or D airport and below 2,500 AGL
- beneath class B shelves
- within class B VFR corridors

200 KIAS

## Exceptions

[91.117]()

Airspeed limits may be broken if an operation has a minimum safe airspeed that is higher.
